Interstellar
Actors Quote
Interstellar is a science fiction movie directed by Christopher Nolan. The story follows a group of astronauts who travel through a wormhole in space to find a new habitable planet for humanity because Earth is slowly becoming unlivable. The movie beautifully combines science, emotions, sacrifice, and the bond between a father and daughter.
Matthew McConaughey as Cooper, Anne Hathaway as Amelia Brand, Jessica Chastain as Murph, and Michael Caine as Professor Brand delivered outstanding performances in the movie.
